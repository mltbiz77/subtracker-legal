Privacy Policy for SubTracker Live

Effective Date: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Nocte Ventures Ltd (registered in England and Wales) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use SubTracker Live (the "App").

Privacy First: SubTracker Live is designed with your privacy as our top priority. All your subscription data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

SubTracker Live stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Subscription names and amounts you enter
  • Billing cycles and renewal dates
  • Subscription categories and preferences
  • Your selected currency settings
  • App configuration and usage settings

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal identification or payment information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal contact information
  • Credit card or financial account details
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

3. How We Use Information

All data processing happens locally on your device. We use this information solely to:

  • Display your subscription information within the app
  • Calculate spending totals across different time periods
  • Schedule local notification reminders for renewals
  • Maintain your app preferences and settings

4. Data Security

Your subscription data is stored locally using Apple's Core Data framework with system-level encryption and access controls. No data is transmitted to outside servers or organizations.

5. User Rights

You have full control over your data. You can view, edit, or delete any subscription information within the app. All data is automatically removed when you uninstall the App.

6. Third-Party Services

SubTracker Live does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline with no external integrations.

7. In-App Purchases

SubTracker Live Pro is processed through Apple's App Store. We only receive notification of your Pro status - no payment details or personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at hello@nocteventures.com.
