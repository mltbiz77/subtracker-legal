Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Privacy First: SubTracker is designed with your privacy as our top priority. All your subscription data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally

SubTracker stores the following information locally on your device using Core Data:

  • Subscription names and amounts you enter
  • Billing cycles and renewal dates
  • Subscription categories you select
  • Currency preferences
  • App settings and preferences

1.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

SubTracker does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Payment card details or financial account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Contact lists or device data

2. How We Use Your Information

The data you enter into SubTracker is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying your subscription information within the app
  • Calculating spending totals across different time periods
  • Scheduling local notification reminders for renewals
  • Maintaining your app preferences and settings

All processing occurs locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your subscription data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework, which provides:

  • Encrypted storage on your device
  • Protection through iOS security features
  • No cloud synchronization or backup

3.2 Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You manually delete subscriptions within the app
  • You delete the SubTracker app from your device
  • You reset or restore your device

4. Notifications

SubTracker uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals. These notifications:

  • Are scheduled and displayed locally on your device
  • Do not involve external push notification services
  • Require your explicit permission through iOS notification settings
  • Can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings

5. In-App Purchases

SubTracker offers in-app purchases for Pro features. All purchase transactions are:

  • Processed directly by Apple through the App Store
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy and terms
  • Not accessible to us beyond knowing your Pro status
  • Protected by Apple's security and privacy standards

6. Third-Party Services

SubTracker does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks or trackers
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage or synchronization services

7. Children's Privacy

SubTracker is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to our app, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

SubTracker supports multiple currencies and is available internationally. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no cross-border data transfers occur.

9. Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data in SubTracker:

  • Access: View all your data within the app interface
  • Modify: Edit or update any subscription information
  • Delete: Remove individual subscriptions or all data by deleting the app
  • Export: Currently not supported, but data remains accessible within the app

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Any changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Communicated through app updates when significant changes occur
  • Effective immediately upon posting

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or SubTracker's privacy practices, please contact us through:

  • The support option within the SubTracker app
  • App Store reviews (we monitor and respond to privacy concerns)

App Developer: SubTracker Team
Jurisdiction: This privacy policy is governed by applicable data protection laws.

Remember: Your privacy is paramount to us. SubTracker was built specifically to keep your financial information private and secure on your own device.
